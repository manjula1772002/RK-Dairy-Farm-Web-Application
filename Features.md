# 🏡 **RK Dairy Farm**
## 🥛 Project Overview

**RK Dairy Farm** is a simple and user-friendly web application designed to provide fresh and natural dairy products directly to customers.

The platform allows users to easily browse available products and securely purchase them after logging into their account. It aims to make accessing high-quality dairy products convenient, reliable, and efficient.
## 🎯 Problem Statement

- Difficulty in finding fresh and chemical-free dairy products  
- Lack of a digital platform for dairy farm management

## 💡 Solution

We developed a web application that:

- Connects customers directly to the dairy farm  
- Provides fresh dairy products online  
- Allows easy browsing and ordering  
- Includes a secure login system
